Best View Guest House Booking Policy

At Best View Guest House, we strive to provide a smooth and enjoyable booking experience for all our guests. Please review our booking policy below:

1. Booking Process

  • Reservation Requests: Bookings can be requested online via our website, email, WhatsApp, or phone. All booking requests are subject to availability.
  • Confirmation: Your booking will only be confirmed once you receive a formal confirmation email or message from us.
  • Check-in and Check-out Times:
    • Check-in: From 2:00 PM
    • Check-out: By 10:00 AM
  • Guest Limits: Each unit accommodates a maximum of 2 adults and 2 children under the age of 13. Please specify the number of guests when booking.

2. Payment Terms

  • Deposits: A 50% deposit is required to secure your booking. The remaining balance must be paid at least 48 hours before check-in.
  • Payment Methods: Payments can be made via EFT or bank deposit. Payment details will be shared upon booking confirmation.
  • Failure to Pay: If the deposit or final payment is not made on time, the booking will be canceled.

3. Cancellation Policy

  • More than 14 Days Before Arrival: Full refund of deposit.
  • 7-14 Days Before Arrival: 50% refund of deposit.
  • Less than 7 Days Before Arrival: No refund of deposit.
  • No-shows: No refunds will be issued for no-shows.

4. Changes to Bookings

Date changes are subject to availability and must be requested at least 7 days before your scheduled arrival. An administrative fee may apply.

5. Special Requests

Special requests, such as late check-in or check-out, will be accommodated where possible and must be arranged in advance. Additional fees may apply.

6. House Rules

  • Best View Guest House is a smoke-free property. Smoking is only allowed in designated outdoor areas.
  • No pets are allowed on the property.
  • Quiet hours are between 10:00 PM and 7:00 AM to ensure a peaceful environment for all guests.

7. Liability

  • Best View Guest House is not responsible for loss, theft, or damage to guests' personal belongings.
  • Guests are responsible for any damage to property caused during their stay.

8. Privacy

All guest information will be handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy and will not be shared with third parties.

By confirming a booking, you agree to abide by this policy. For questions or further assistance, please contact us at 082 464 4791 or email info@bestviewstay.co.za.
